01
NAP Consistency Rules
Name · Address · Phone — the foundation of all local SEO. These 3 fields must be byte-for-byte identical everywhere.
⚠️
NAP inconsistency is the #1 reason local businesses don't rank. The scraper captures NAP as it exists on Google, Yelp, and the client's site. Discrepancies are flagged as audit findings — these are your sales pain points and billable fixes. Every output in this system uses the canonical NAP fields below. Never use a different format in any two places.
Canonical NAP Fields — Master Definitions
NAME → business_name
Full legal or DBA name exactly as filed
ADDRESS → address_street + address_city + address_state + address_zip
Never abbreviate — 123 Main Street, not 123 Main St
PHONE → phone_business
Format: (501) 555-1234 — consistent everywhere
WEBSITE → website_url
Include https:// — no trailing slash

From prospect discovery to live client — every step, what triggers it, what it produces.
1
Scraper Runs on Prospect
You visit a prospect's GBP/Yelp/website in Chrome. One click — plugin scrapes all AUTO fields, computes NAP consistency, pulls audit data. Fires GHL webhook → creates prospect contact + tags. Generates cold call brief PDF automatically.
AUTOCold Call Brief GeneratedGHL Contact Created

You Make the Cold Call
Brief is on your screen. You know their rating (3.2⭐), last review (4 months ago), mobile score (38/100), and that their NAP is inconsistent across 3 platforms before they say hello. You close. They agree to onboard.
YOU
3
Onboarding Form Sent to Client
You send client the 501.business/gold link. Form auto-loads with their scraped data pre-filled. They confirm/correct Part 1, then fill Parts 2–6 manually. Takes ~8 minutes. On submit → GHL webhook fires → all 60+ custom fields populate.
AUTO + CLIENTAll Fields Populated in GHL

Cowork Triggers: KB Compile + BluePage Draft
GHL webhook receipt triggers Cowork. Cowork pulls all custom fields → compiles knowledge base document → generates Oracle prompt → creates BluePage HTML from master template with all fields injected → saves as draft → sends you a preview link + approval request.
