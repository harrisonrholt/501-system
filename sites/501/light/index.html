What happens if I stop paying?
›
Your BluePage goes offline automatically the day your subscription lapses. Your listing on our SEO directory network is removed. The moment you restart, everything comes back live within 24 hours. No data is deleted for 90 days.
Do I own the page?
›
You own your business information and content — we host it on our domain network (501.business/yourslug). Think of it like a storefront in our building. You have full control of your info, your photos, your brand. The infrastructure is ours, which is what keeps your monthly cost affordable instead of $500+/month.
How is this different from a regular website?
›
A regular website is built to be read. A BluePage is built to be tapped. It's designed for the 8 seconds a customer has on their phone — they call, book, save your number, or leave a review without scrolling through paragraphs. And it's live in 24 hours, not 6 weeks.
What if I already have a website?
›
Perfect — your BluePage works alongside it. Most contractors use both: the BluePage is what they text to customers and put on truck magnets, the website is what shows up on Google. They serve different jobs. You can also link your existing site from the BluePage.
How long does it take to build?
›
24 hours from the time you submit your info form and payment is confirmed. Rush builds (same day) are available for an additional $97 — ask Harrison during the call.
What do I have to do to maintain it?
›
Nothing. That's the whole point. 501 Digital handles hosting, updates, and maintenance. If you need something changed — new phone number, new service added, new photos — you text Harrison and it's done within 48 hours at no extra charge.
