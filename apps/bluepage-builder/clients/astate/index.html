What is Red Iron Steel?
Red iron refers to heavy-gauge structural I-beam steel — the standard of the commercial construction industry. More rigid, more durable, and longer-lasting than lighter-gauge alternatives. When you build with red iron, you build once.
🏭
Why Red Iron Over Alternatives
Lighter gauge buildings may cost less upfront and look similar in a brochure. Red iron structures hold their value, meet higher code standards, and perform for decades with minimal maintenance. Our customers rarely need to ask twice.
